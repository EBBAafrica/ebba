About EBBA

Meet EBBA

We, the founding members of Elevated Beyond Building Africa (EBBA), recognize the need to harness the creative, intellectual, and entrepreneurial potential of Zimbabwean youth, particularly those in high schools and tertiary institutions.

Acknowledging that young people are not only the leaders of tomorrow but the architects of Africa’s present and future, we hereby establish this Constitution to govern a youth-led, youth-driven association committed to empowering, nurturing, promoting, creating, and championing inclusive development. Rooted in inclusivity, innovation, and integrity, EBBA is committed to elevating young people to realize their full potential.

Our Vision

To empower Zimbabwean youth by unlocking and nurturing their creative, academic, and entrepreneurial potential, envisioning a future where every young person transforms their passion into opportunity through innovation, digital tools, and mentorship.

Our Mission

To cultivate a generation of confident, skilled, and self-sustaining young Zimbabweans who use their talents in art, culture, public speaking, and digital media to shape their futures and contribute meaningfully to national and continental development.

Strategic Goals

  • Provide inclusive platforms for talent development.
  • Transform talents into income opportunities.
  • Promote digital literacy and innovation.
  • Offer mentorship and capacity-building.
  • Support SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth.
  • Amplify marginalized youth voices.
  • Foster pan-African youth identity.
  • Maintain non-partisan, youth-led leadership.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We equip youth with the tools, skills, and confidence to lead, create, and drive impact.

Leadership

We nurture the leadership potential of every young person through mentorship, service, and responsibility.

Inclusion

We promote diversity and equitable access regardless of gender, background, or ability.

Innovation

We champion forward-thinking solutions and the use of digital tools to address youth challenges.

Creativity

We honour originality and support the transformation of creative ideas into real-world change.

Integrity

We uphold transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership in all decisions and actions.

Our Programs

Youth Leadership, Advocacy & Empowerment

Focuses on civic participation, life skills, youth rights, SDG education, public speaking, and mentorship.

Mandate:

  • Equip youth with leadership, public speaking, and self-mastery skills.
  • Promote civic responsibility and positive national identity.
  • Train youth in digital advocacy, policy dialogue, and grassroots mobilization.

Flagship Activities:

  • Next Gen Zimbabwe Championships (Debate, quiz, public speaking)
  • Leadership Bootcamps (Soft skills, teamwork, mentorship)
  • Advocacy Masterclasses (Tackling drug abuse, mental health, gender equality)
  • Civic Roadshows (Awareness campaigns in schools and communities)
  • “My Africa” Week (Black pride and Pan-African consciousness events)

Talent & Creative Innovation

Focuses on artistic, literary, performance, fashion, and digital storytelling talents.

Mandate:

  • Empower youth in visual and performing arts, fashion, literature, and content creation.
  • Reclaim and modernize African cultural identity through creative youth-led platforms.
  • Enable creators to monetize their skills through exhibitions, publications, and digital platforms.

Flagship Activities:

  • Canvas Clash (National art & fashion design competition)
  • ZimSpark Creative Gala (Annual youth talent and innovation show)
  • VIBEfest (Music and dance festival for high schools)
  • Express Me Bootcamp (Digital content creation & storytelling)
  • Youth Author Publishing Program (End-to-end support for young writers)
  • “Echoes of Ubuntu” (Afrocentric art & fashion showcase)
  • Gogo Neni Chronicles (African storytelling revival events)

Digital Skills, Education & Entrepreneurship

Focuses on digital literacy, exam prep, tech use (AI, publishing, platforms), and youth-owned businesses.

Mandate:

  • Prepare youth to excel academically and thrive in the digital economy.
  • Support income generation through digital tools and entrepreneurship.
  • Develop innovative thinkers with practical business and tech skills.

Flagship Activities:

  • “How to Pass ZIMSEC” eBook Series + WhatsApp Revision Hubs
  • Study Bootcamps (Peer tutoring + mock exam simulations)
  • Side Hustle Masterclass (Youth-led business incubation sessions)
  • Zimbabwe’s Next Big Thing (Innovation tournaments)
  • Artist Marketplace & Publishing Platforms (Youth authors and designers go digital)
  • Partnerships with SME platforms, Youth Business Trust, and AI tools for book/art production.
